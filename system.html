Gross Margin Comparison — Selected Companies, 2024
The widest spread in the US healthcare system: from Cencora at 3.5% (drug distributor) to AstraZeneca at 82% (branded pharma).

Drug distributors operate on 3–4% gross margins as pass-through logistics businesses. Diagnostics labs (Quest, Labcorp) and GPOs (Premier) run at 31–62% margins on a much smaller revenue base. Eli Lilly's 81% reflects GLP-1 drug pricing power (Mounjaro, Zepbound).

Hospital Supply & Service — Sector Breakdown
The sector is structurally two businesses: drug distribution ($830B, 3–4% margins) and everything else ($74B, 21–62% margins). The Big 3 distributors move nearly all branded and generic pharmaceuticals from manufacturers to US hospitals, pharmacies, and clinics.
