Medicare Advantage Growth — All Payers
MA enrollment as % of total Medicare (CMS data). MA now covers nearly half of all Medicare beneficiaries.

Medicare Advantage payments to insurers averaged $14,000 per enrollee in 2024 — 22% above traditional Medicare per-capita spending. MedPAC estimates MA overpayment at $80–90B annually.

Gross Margin Trends
Gross margin reflects premium revenue less medical costs paid out. A lower margin = higher medical loss ratio. 2024 saw pressure on Humana and UNH from elevated utilization and Medicare Advantage cost increases.

Cigna's 2024 gross margin drop reflects the Evernorth PBM segment growing faster than insurance — PBM revenue is largely pass-through with thin margins (2–5%), pulling down the blended rate.
